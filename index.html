Favourite food is Biryani

Chicken biryani is a delicious Pakistani/Indian rice dish that's typically reserved for special occasions such as weddings, parties, or holidays such as Ramadan. It has a lengthy preparation, but the work is definitely worth it. For biryani, basmati rice is the ideal variety to use.

Favourite heroine is Kalpana chawla

Kalpana Chawla was the first Indian-born female astronaut to go to space. She was an aeronautical engineer with many patents and had won many awards. She had a master's and doctoral degree in Aerospace Engineering from the University of Texas at Arlington, USA.

A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, in full Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, (born October 15, 1931, Rameswaram, India—died July 27, 2015, Shillong), Indian scientist and politician who played a leading role in the development of India's missile and nuclear weapons programs. He was president of India from 2002 to 2007
